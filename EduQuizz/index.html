EduQuizz

EduQuizz GUIDELINES

  • Each quiz will have a time limit which will be indicated.
  • The timer for each quiz is located at the top left of the screen.
  • When your time runs out, the quiz ends and you will have to reload the page to re-attempt it.
  • At the end of the quiz page is a "See Score" button which will display how much out of the total you got. It will also blink the correct answers you got green.
  • The "See Score" button only works once all questions have been attempted. However once you have submited, you are allowed to change answers without a time limit.
  • Also at the end of the quiz page is a "Go to Related Note Page" button which will take you to the Edukatte note page for that respective quiz.
