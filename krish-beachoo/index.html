Krish Beachoo

Founder | Primary Contributor | Web Master

Number of Resources: 141

Meet Krish Beachoo, a multifaceted individual who has left an indelible mark in the realms of education and technology. Krish embarked on his academic journey at Pranava Educational Institute, where he successfully navigated the S.E.A examination in 2018. Currently pursuing management, mathematics, and economics at Naparima College under the CAPE program, Krish has not only excelled in his studies but has also demonstrated exceptional leadership and entrepreneurial skills.

As the President of the Naparima College Business Club, Krish has carved a niche for himself in the business domain. His achievements include securing 4th place at the oration competition in 2022 and claiming 2nd place at the prestigious Arthur Lok Jack Global School of Business' Business Simulation Competition. Krish is not only an academic achiever but also an active participant in debates, showcasing his prowess in public speaking.

Beyond academics and business, Krish is a tech-savvy innovator. He founded Edukatte and serves as its Founder, Web Master, and Primary Contributor. Additionally, he manages ResourcesCompile and Krish Clock, both of which he designed in 2019 using the Notion database. Krish's brainchild, Krish Notes, evolved into Edukatte, a coded website that has become a valuable resource for learners. With over 250 notes across almost every notebook on the platform, Krish stands as the most prolific contributor at Edukatte.

Known for his unwavering work ethic, Krish is particularly adept at collaborating within a team. His dedication and proficiency extend beyond the traditional academic sphere, making him a standout individual with a passion for education, business, and technology.
