Letter Formats


by Bryan Balkissoon on 31 Jul 2023

Image: https://unsplash.com/@hudsoncrafted
Edu Level: CSEC,NCSE


Informal Letter

(Used for letters to chat, to share ideas, to thank, invite, or congratulate a friend/relative)

Your Address:

-﻿----

Date:

-﻿----

Salutation:

(Dear …)

Body of Letter:

  • Each new paragraph indented.

Closing:

  • Only first word capitalised.

Eg. Yours sincerely,

Jacob

Formal Letter

(Generally used for Letters of Complaint, Apology, Permission, Request, Formal Invitation)

Sender’s Address (Return Address):

-﻿----

Date:

-﻿----

Recipient’s Address (Inside Address):

  • Name of Recipient
  • Title/Position of Recipient
  • Company/Institution
  • Full Address

-﻿----

Salutation

  • Dear Sir/Madam (Unknown)
  • Dear Mr/Mrs/Miss (known)

Subject

  • Subject:
  • Must be underlined.

Eg. Subject: Improper Waste Disposal in Neighbourhood

Body of Letter

  • Each new paragraph indented.

Closing:

  • Only first word capitalised.
  • Include Title

Eg. Yours sincerely

Jacob Martin

Student

----- College

Letter to the Editor

Sender’s Address

-﻿----

Date

-﻿----

Recipient’s Address:

The Editor

Newspaper Name

Newspaper Address

Body:

  • Each paragraph indented

Dear Sir/Madam,

  • Intro Paragraph: Attention grabber; thesis statement
  • Body Paragraphs:
  • Problem/Complaint/Solution
  • Evidence to Support
  • Explanation of Evidence
  • Drawn Conclusion
  • Conclusion: Reiterate thesis statement; recommendation/suggestion (if necessary)

Closure

  • Full Signature

Eg. Yours respectfully,

Jacob Martin.
